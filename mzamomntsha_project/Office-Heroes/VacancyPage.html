Job Title: Secondary School Mathematics Teacher

We are looking for an experienced Mathematics Teacher to join our team.

Job Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics or related field
  • At least 3 years of teaching experience
  • Strong knowledge of Mathematics curriculum for secondary schools
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work with diverse groups of students and colleagues
  • Experience with modern teaching methods, such as project-based learning and flipped classroom

Job Description:

  • We are seeking a highly motivated and experienced Mathematics Teacher to join our secondary school teaching team.
  • The successful candidate will be responsible for delivering high-quality instruction to our students and promoting a positive learning environment.
  • This position requires strong communication skills, the ability to work well with others, and a passion for teaching Mathematics.
  • The ideal candidate should have experience with modern teaching methods, such as project-based learning and flipped classroom, and be familiar with current Mathematics curriculum for secondary schools.


  • To apply for this position, please submit your CV and a cover letter highlighting your qualifications and teaching philosophy. Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview.
